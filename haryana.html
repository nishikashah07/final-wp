Top Places to Visit in Haryana

Kurukshetra

A significant historical and religious site, known for being the battleground of the Mahabharata.

Pinjore Gardens

Beautiful Mughal-style gardens near Panchkula, perfect for picnics and serene walks.

Sultanpur National Park

A haven for birdwatchers, with migratory birds arriving from as far as Siberia and Europe.

Surajkund

Famous for its annual international crafts fair and historical reservoir.

Morni Hills

The only hill station in Haryana, offering scenic views, trekking, and outdoor activities.

Panipat

Known for its rich historical significance, particularly the three major battles that shaped Indian history.

Faridabad

Home to the popular Surajkund Crafts Mela and several modern parks and attractions.

Damdama Lake

A perfect spot for adventure enthusiasts, offering boating, rock climbing, and picnic areas.

Rohtak

Known for the Tilyar Lake and its adjacent wildlife sanctuary and parks.

Yamunanagar

Famous for its scenic beauty, lush forests, and the ancient Kalesar National Park.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Haryana is well connected by rail, road, and air. Major cities like Gurgaon and Faridabad are part of the National Capital Region, with easy access to Delhi's Indira Gandhi International Airport.

Stay: Options range from luxury resorts in Gurgaon to budget hotels and guesthouses in smaller towns.

Popular Delicacies: Don't miss trying local dishes like Bajra Khichdi, Bathua Raita, and the famous Haryana-style Lassi.
