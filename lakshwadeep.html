Top Places to Visit in Lakshadweep

Kavaratti

The capital of Lakshadweep, known for its pristine beaches, clear lagoons, and water sports.

Minicoy

A picturesque island known for its crescent-shaped beach, lighthouse, and unique culture.

Agatti Island

Famous for its coral reefs, turquoise waters, and excellent snorkeling and diving opportunities.

Bangaram Island

An uninhabited island perfect for a secluded escape with stunning beaches and clear waters.

Kadmat Island

Known for its long sandy beaches, lagoon, and a popular destination for scuba diving.

Kalpeni Island

Offers water sports and has a stunning lagoon ideal for kayaking and snorkeling.

Andretti Island

The largest island in Lakshadweep, known for its natural beauty and rich marine life.

Amini Island

Famous for its coral craft and traditional coconut fiber products, offering a cultural experience.

Chetlat Island

A quiet island known for its fishing and breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea.

Bitra Island

The smallest inhabited island, known for its peaceful surroundings and bird-watching opportunities.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Lakshadweep can be accessed via flights to Agatti Airport or by ships from Kochi. Boats and ferries connect different islands.

Stay: Accommodation options include government guesthouses and eco-friendly beach resorts.

Popular Delicacies: Try local seafood dishes, coconut preparations, and fish curries.
