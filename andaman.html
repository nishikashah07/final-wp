Top Places to Visit in Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Radhanagar Beach

Renowned for its white sand and turquoise blue waters, perfect for a serene getaway.

Cellular Jail

A historic site known for its significance during India's freedom struggle.

Havelock Island

Popular for scuba diving, snorkeling, and beautiful beaches like Elephant Beach.

Ross Island

Once the administrative headquarters, now a fascinating ruin with historical remnants.

Baratang Island

Known for its limestone caves, mud volcanoes, and dense mangrove forests.

Neil Island

A tranquil island perfect for relaxation, known for its coral reefs and clear waters.

North Bay Island

Famous for underwater activities like snorkeling and sea walking.

Chidiya Tapu

Ideal for birdwatching and stunning sunset views over the Andaman Sea.

Maya Bandar

A great spot for fishing and exploring the marine life of the Andaman Sea.

Barren Island

Home to the only active volcano in South Asia, offering unique sightseeing opportunities.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Andaman and Nicobar Islands can be reached via direct flights to Port Blair. Ferries connect different islands.

Stay: Accommodation ranges from luxury resorts in Havelock and Port Blair to budget-friendly guesthouses in Neil Island.

Popular Delicacies: Try local seafood, coconut prawn curry, and Andaman fish curry.
