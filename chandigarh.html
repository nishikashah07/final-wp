Top Places to Visit in Chandigarh

Rock Garden

A unique garden made from industrial and urban waste, showcasing stunning sculptures and art installations.

Sukhna Lake

A serene man-made lake perfect for boating, walking, and enjoying beautiful sunsets.

Zakir Hussain Rose Garden

Asia's largest rose garden, featuring over 1,600 different species of roses and a variety of other plants.

Mansa Devi Temple

A popular Hindu temple situated on the top of the Mansa Devi hill, offering panoramic views of Chandigarh.

Chandigarh High Court

Designed by Le Corbusier, this stunning building is an example of modern architecture and urban planning.

Elante Mall

A major shopping destination with a wide range of shops, dining options, and entertainment facilities.

Government Museum and Art Gallery

A museum housing an extensive collection of Gandharan art, paintings, and other historical artifacts.

Pinjore Gardens

A historic garden with beautiful Mughal architecture, terraced lawns, and cascading fountains.

Northern Railway Golden Jubilee Park

A well-maintained park perfect for leisurely strolls and family picnics, adorned with lush greenery.

Chhatbir Zoo

A large zoo featuring a variety of animals, including lions, tigers, and various birds, ideal for family outings.
