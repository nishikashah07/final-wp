Top Places to Visit in Uttarakhand

Dehradun

The capital city known for its pleasant weather and beautiful landscapes.

Nainital

A charming hill station famous for its lakes and scenic views.

Mussoorie

Known as the 'Queen of the Hills', it offers stunning views and lush greenery.

Rishikesh

A spiritual town on the banks of the Ganges, known for yoga and adventure sports.

Haridwar

One of the holiest cities in India, famous for the Ganga Aarti.

Kedarnath

A sacred pilgrimage site known for the Kedarnath Temple, set in the Himalayas.

Badrinath

Another important pilgrimage site, part of the Char Dham Yatra.

Auli

A ski resort town known for its slopes and stunning views of the Himalayas.

Valley of Flowers

A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its vibrant alpine flowers.

Jim Corbett National Park

India's first national park, known for its diverse wildlife and scenic beauty.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Uttarakhand is well connected by road, rail, and air. The nearest airport is in Dehradun, and major towns are accessible by bus.

Stay: Accommodation options range from luxury hotels in Nainital and Dehradun to budget guesthouses and homestays.

Popular Delicacies: Don't miss trying local dishes like Aloo Ke Gutke, Kumaoni Raita, and Bal Mithai.
