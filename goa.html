Top Places to Visit in Goa

Baga Beach

A popular beach known for its nightlife, water sports, and beach shacks.

Calangute Beach

Known as the "Queen of Beaches," it is perfect for sunbathing and shopping.

Fort Aguada

A 17th-century Portuguese fort with a lighthouse and stunning sea views.

Dudhsagar Falls

One of the tallest waterfalls in India, known for its breathtaking beauty.

Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, famous for housing the remains of St. Francis Xavier.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Goa is accessible by air via Dabolim Airport, and by train with stations like Madgaon and Thivim. Local transport includes rental scooters, taxis, and buses.

Stay: Accommodation options range from luxurious beach resorts to budget guesthouses and hostels near popular beaches.
