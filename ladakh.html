Top Places to Visit in Ladakh

Pangong Lake

A stunning high-altitude lake known for its vibrant blue waters and breathtaking scenery.

Nubra Valley

A picturesque valley known for its sand dunes, double-humped camels, and stunning landscapes.

Leh Palace

An ancient palace that offers panoramic views of Leh and the surrounding mountains.

Shanti Stupa

A beautiful white dome-shaped stupa that promotes peace and offers stunning views of the sunset.

Thiksey Monastery

A prominent Tibetan Buddhist monastery known for its beautiful architecture and serene atmosphere.

Hemis Monastery

The largest monastery in Ladakh, famous for its annual festival and stunning surroundings.

Kargil

A town known for its rich history, beautiful landscapes, and as a base for trekking and adventure.

Zanskar Valley

A remote and stunning valley famous for its trekking routes and unique landscapes.

Suru Valley

A beautiful valley known for its lush greenery, picturesque mountains, and adventure opportunities.

Magnetic Hill

A famous hill known for its optical illusion where vehicles seem to roll uphill against gravity.
