Top Places to Visit in Sikkim

Gangtok

The capital city, known for its vibrant culture and stunning views of the Himalayas.

Pelling

Famous for its breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga mountain range.

Namchi

Known for its beautiful monasteries and the giant statue of Guru Padmasambhava.

Lachung

A picturesque village, known for its scenic beauty and proximity to Yumthang Valley.

Zero Point

Offers mesmerizing views and is a great spot for snow activities in winter.

Khecheopalri Lake

A sacred lake surrounded by lush forests, known for its serene beauty.

Yuksom

Known for its rich history and as the starting point for trekking to Kanchenjunga.

Zuluk

A small village famous for its winding roads and panoramic views of the mountains.

Sikkim Rhododendron Sanctuary

Famous for its diverse species of rhododendrons and beautiful trekking trails.

Rabdentse Ruins

The historical site of the ancient capital of Sikkim, offering great views and history.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Sikkim is well connected by road, and the nearest airport is in Pakyong. Gangtok can be reached by taxi or bus from Siliguri.

Stay: Options range from luxury hotels in Gangtok to cozy homestays in Pelling and Lachung.

Popular Delicacies: Don't miss trying local dishes like Gundruk, Phagshapa, and the famous Sikkimese noodles.
