Top Places to Visit in Odisha

Puri

Home to the famous Jagannath Temple and pristine beaches, Puri is a major pilgrimage site.

Konark

Known for the UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Sun Temple, with intricate architectural beauty.

Bhubaneswar

The capital city known for ancient temples, including the famous Lingaraj Temple.

Chilika Lake

Asia's largest brackish water lagoon, famous for migratory birds and scenic views.

Cuttack

A city with rich heritage, known for the Barabati Fort and unique silver filigree work.

Dhenkanal

Known for its scenic beauty, wildlife sanctuaries, and the Kapilas Hill Temple.

Simlipal National Park

A major tiger reserve and biosphere, perfect for nature and wildlife enthusiasts.

Gopalpur

A beautiful beach town with serene beaches, ideal for a peaceful getaway.

Sambalpur

Known for the Hirakud Dam, the longest dam in India, and traditional Sambalpuri handicrafts.

Ratnagiri

An ancient Buddhist site with monasteries, stupas, and beautiful archaeological sites.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Odisha is well connected by rail, road, and air. Bhubaneswar has an international airport. Local travel options include buses, taxis, and auto-rickshaws.

Stay: Accommodation options range from heritage hotels to beach resorts and budget lodgings. Popular places to stay include Puri beach area and near the Jagannath Temple.

Popular Delicacies: Try traditional dishes like Pakhala Bhata, Dalma, and sweets like Chhena Poda and Rasagola.
