Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Chhattisgarh is well connected by road and rail. Raipur has an airport with flights to major cities.

Stay: Accommodation options range from luxury hotels to budget guesthouses in major cities like Raipur and Bilaspur.

Popular Delicacies: Don't miss trying local dishes like Chana Samosa, Farcha, and the traditional rice dishes.
