Top Places to Visit in Rajasthan

Jaipur

Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is famous for its royal palaces and vibrant markets.

Udaipur

Famed for its beautiful lakes and palaces, Udaipur offers stunning views and rich history.

Jaisalmer

Known as the Golden City, Jaisalmer is famous for its desert forts and stunning sand dunes.

Jodhpur

Known as the Blue City, Jodhpur is famous for its historic forts and vibrant blue houses.

Pushkar

A sacred city famous for its annual camel fair and the Pushkar Lake.

Ranthambore National Park

One of the largest national parks in India, known for its tiger reserve.

Chittorgarh

Famous for its massive fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, with a rich history.

Bikaner

Known for its rich heritage, delicious snacks, and the Junagarh Fort.

Alwar

Known for its scenic beauty and the famous Bala Quila Fort.

Sawai Madhopur

Gateway to the Ranthambore National Park, perfect for wildlife enthusiasts.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Rajasthan is well connected by rail, road, and air. Major cities like Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jodhpur have airports. For local travel, taxis and auto-rickshaws are widely available.

Stay: Options range from luxurious heritage hotels to budget guesthouses. Popular areas to stay include the City Palace area in Udaipur and near the Hawa Mahal in Jaipur.

Popular Delicacies: Don't miss trying local dishes like Dal Baati Churma, Gatte Ki Sabzi, and Rajasthani Thali. Sweets like Ghevar and Rasgulla are a must-try!
