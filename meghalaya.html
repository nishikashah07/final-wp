Top Places to Visit in Meghalaya

Shillong

Known as the Scotland of the East, Shillong is famous for its hills, lakes, and vibrant culture.

Cherrapunji

Famous for its living root bridges, waterfalls, and lush landscapes, once the wettest place on Earth.

Mawlynnong

Known as Asia's cleanest village, Mawlynnong offers beautiful scenery and rich cultural experiences.

Dawki

Famous for its crystal-clear river, Dawki is perfect for boating and exploring the scenic countryside.

Mawsmai Caves

A fascinating limestone cave system that offers a unique underground adventure.

Umngot River

Known for its stunningly clear waters, this river allows you to see right through to the riverbed.

Nongriat

Famous for its double-decker root bridge, Nongriat offers incredible trekking and scenic beauty.

Laitlum Canyon

A breathtaking viewpoint offering panoramic views of valleys, perfect for nature lovers.

Elephant Falls

A popular waterfall near Shillong, named for a rock resembling an elephant.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Meghalaya is accessible by road from Assam. Shillong Airport offers limited flights. For local travel, taxis and shared cabs are widely available.

Stay: Options range from eco-lodges to budget hotels in Shillong and Cherrapunji.

Popular Delicacies: Enjoy local dishes like Jadoh (rice with pork), Dohneiiong (pork curry), and the flavorful Tungrymbai (fermented soybeans).
