Top Places to Visit in Kerala

Alleppey

Famous for its backwaters and houseboat cruises, a perfect place to relax.

Kumarakom

A serene backwater destination known for its bird sanctuary and houseboats.

Kochi

A bustling port city with a rich history and a blend of cultures, known for its Chinese fishing nets.

Munnar

A picturesque hill station famous for its tea plantations and stunning landscapes.

Thekkady

Home to the Periyar National Park, ideal for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Varkala

Known for its stunning cliffs, beaches, and Ayurvedic health resorts.

Wayanad

A scenic district with lush greenery, waterfalls, and wildlife sanctuaries.

Bekal

Famous for its historic Bekal Fort and beautiful beaches.

Thrissur

Known for its cultural heritage and the famous Thrissur Pooram festival.

Kollam

A charming coastal town known for its cashew trade and backwaters.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Kerala is well connected by air, rail, and road. Kochi and Trivandrum have international airports. Local transportation includes taxis, auto-rickshaws, and boats.

Stay: Options range from luxury resorts to budget homestays and guesthouses. Backwater resorts in Alleppey and Kumarakom are particularly popular.

Popular Delicacies: Don't miss trying traditional Kerala dishes like Appam, Puttu, and Karimeen Pollichathu, along with sweets like Payasam and Ada Pradhaman.
