Top Places to Visit in Jammu and Kashmir

Srinagar

The summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, famous for its beautiful gardens, houseboats, and Dal Lake.

Gulmarg

A popular hill station and skiing destination known for its stunning landscapes and lush meadows.

Pahalgam

A scenic town located at the confluence of Lidder River, known for trekking and outdoor activities.

Sonmarg

A beautiful meadow known for its breathtaking views and as a base for treks to nearby glaciers.

Kashmir Valley

A picturesque valley surrounded by the Himalayas, known for its stunning landscapes and rich culture.

Leh

The capital of Ladakh, famous for its Buddhist monasteries, stunning mountain views, and rich culture.

Amarnath Cave

A famous pilgrimage site known for its ice Shiva Lingam, attracting thousands of devotees annually.

Betaab Valley

A picturesque valley known for its lush greenery and serene environment, popular for Bollywood films.

Tangmarg

A charming town near Gulmarg, known for its scenic beauty and as a gateway to the Gulmarg ski area.

Pulwama

Famous for its beautiful gardens and saffron fields, known as the 'Saffron City' of Kashmir.
