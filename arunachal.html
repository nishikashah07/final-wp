Top Places to Visit in Arunachal Pradesh

Tawang

Known for its stunning monastery and scenic beauty, Tawang is a spiritual and cultural hub.

Itanagar

The capital city, known for Ita Fort, Ganga Lake, and its vibrant tribal culture.

Ziro

Famous for its picturesque valley, unique Apatani tribal culture, and the Ziro Music Festival.

Bomdila

A charming town with beautiful views of the Himalayas and rich Buddhist heritage.

Pasighat

The oldest town in Arunachal Pradesh, known for its scenic beauty and adventure sports.

Roing

Known for its stunning lakes, valleys, and the rich Idu Mishmi tribal culture.

Namdapha National Park

A biodiversity hotspot, this park is known for its unique flora and fauna.

Mechuka

Known for its scenic beauty, Mechuka is a remote valley with stunning landscapes.

Sangti Valley

A picturesque valley known for its scenic views and as a haven for birdwatchers.

Dirang

Known for its beautiful valleys, hot springs, and the stunning Dirang Dzong.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Arunachal Pradesh is accessible by road from Assam. The nearest airports are in Tezpur and Guwahati. For local travel, taxis and buses are available.

Stay: Options range from comfortable hotels to guesthouses. Popular areas to stay include near the Tawang Monastery and in the Ziro Valley.

Popular Delicacies: Don't miss trying local dishes like Bamboo Shoot, Pika Pila, and Fish Stew. Sweets like Apong and Rice Cake are a must-try!
