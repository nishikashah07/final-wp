Top Places to Visit in West Bengal

Kolkata

The cultural capital of India, known for its colonial architecture and vibrant arts scene.

Darjeeling

Famous for its tea gardens and stunning views of the Himalayas, especially the Kanchenjunga.

Sundarbans

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its mangrove forests and the Royal Bengal Tiger.

Kalimpong

A hill station known for its beautiful landscapes, monasteries, and vibrant flower markets.

Bankura

Known for its beautiful terracotta temples and rich folk traditions.

Siliguri

A gateway to North-East India, offering diverse culture and access to hill stations.

Murshidabad

Known for its historical significance and the beautiful Hazarduari Palace.

Bishnupur

Famous for its terracotta temples and Baluchari sarees.

Purulia

A picturesque district known for its natural beauty and folk culture.

Digha

A popular beach destination known for its serene coastline and vibrant sunsets.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: West Bengal is well connected by rail, road, and air. Kolkata has a major international airport, and trains and buses are available to various regions.

Stay: Accommodation ranges from luxurious hotels in Kolkata to charming homestays in Darjeeling and eco-resorts in the Sundarbans.

Popular Delicacies: Don't miss trying local dishes like Rosogolla, Mishti Doi, and Fish Curry with rice.
