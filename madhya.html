Top Places to Visit in Madhya Pradesh

Bhopal

The capital city, known for its lakes, rich history, and culture.

Khajuraho

Famous for its stunning temples and intricate erotic sculptures.

Ujjain

An ancient city known for the Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga and Kumbh Mela.

Orchha

A historical town famous for its palaces and temples along the Betwa River.

Pachmarhi

The only hill station in Madhya Pradesh, known for its scenic beauty.

Kanha National Park

One of the largest national parks, known for its diverse wildlife, including tigers.

Bandhavgarh National Park

Famous for its population of tigers and ancient ruins.

Sanchi

Known for its ancient stupas, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Maheshwar

A historical town on the banks of the Narmada River, known for its temples and textiles.

Amarkantak

The origin of the Narmada River, famous for its natural beauty and temples.

Travel and Stay Options

Travel: Madhya Pradesh is well connected by road and rail. Major cities have railway stations and bus services, with an airport in Bhopal.

Stay: Options range from luxurious hotels to budget lodges and heritage properties in cities like Bhopal and Khajuraho.

Popular Delicacies: Don't miss trying local dishes like Poha, Jalebi, and Biryani from Indore, along with sweets like Chikki and Mawa Bati.
